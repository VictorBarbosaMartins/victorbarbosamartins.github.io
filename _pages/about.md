I am an Assistant Professor (Professor Adjunto) at the Physics Institute of the **Federal University of Goiás (UFG)**, Brazil. My research is focused on **Astroparticle Physics** and **Multi-messenger Astronomy**.

My work involves the study of extreme cosmic environments, such as Active Galactic Nuclei and galaxy clusters, using data from the **H.E.S.S.** array, the **IceCube Neutrino Observatory**, and the upcoming **Cherenkov Telescope Array (CTA)**. I am particularly interested in spectral analysis of gamma-ray sources and the development of instrumentation for high-energy astrophysics.

Before joining UFG, I was a postdoctoral researcher at **Ruhr University Bochum** and **DESY** in Germany.
