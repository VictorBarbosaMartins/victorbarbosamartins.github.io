   11 I am a Professor Adjunto at the Physics Institute of the **Federal University of Goiás (UFG)**, Brazil. My research is centered on **Astroparticle Physics** and **Multi-messenger
      Astronomy**, with a particular focus on the study of the most energetic phenomena in the universe.
   12
   13 Research Interests
   14 ---
   15 * **High-Energy Gamma-Ray Astronomy:** Observations of Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN), specifically M87 and the Virgo Cluster, using the H.E.S.S. and CTA observatories.
   16 * **Cosmic Rays:** Investigating the origin and propagation of high-energy particles and their signatures in the intra-cluster medium.
   17 * **Multi-messenger Signatures:** Exploring the links between high-energy neutrinos (IceCube) and gamma-ray sources like Tidal Disruption Events (TDEs) and blazars.
   18 * **Instrumentation & Machine Learning:** Developing structural monitoring systems for telescopes and applying AI to particle detection and analysis.

   28 Background
   29 ---
   30 Before joining UFG, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at **Ruhr University Bochum** (Germany) and **DESY** (Germany). I earned my PhD in Experimental Physics from **Humboldt University of
      Berlin** in 2022, working on the analysis of gamma-ray emission from M87.
