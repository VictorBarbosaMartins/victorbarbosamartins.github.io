    6
    7 I am an Assistant Professor (Professor Adjunto) at the Physics Institute of the **Federal University of Goiás (UFG)**, Brazil. My work lies at the intersection of **Astroparticle Physics**,
      **Multi-messenger Astronomy**, and **Scientific Instrumentation**.
    8
    9 My research focuses on understanding the most extreme environments in the universe, from the vicinity of supermassive black holes in Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) to the diffuse intra-cluster
      medium of galaxy clusters. I am a member of the **H.E.S.S.**, **CTA**, **IceCube**, and **Pierre Auger** collaborations.
   10
   11 Prior to UFG, I held postdoctoral positions at **Ruhr University Bochum** and **DESY** in Germany, where I specialized in very-high-energy gamma-ray analysis and the search for
      multi-messenger signatures of cosmic transients.
