I am an Assistant Professor (Professor Adjunto) at the [Physics Institute](https://if.ufg.br/) of the **Federal University of Goiás (UFG)**, Brazil. I am also a member of the [Graduate Program in Physics (PPG)](https://posgraduacao.if.ufg.br/). I am a **Physical Engineer** and my research is focused on **Astroparticle Physics** and **Multi-messenger Astronomy**.

My work involves analyzing data from various high-energy and multi-wavelength observatories to study extreme cosmic environments. My primary research interests include **multi-messenger astronomy**, **optical polarization**, and the study of **extra-galactic environments**, with a particular focus on **radio galaxies and other types of Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN)**. I also contribute to the development of next-generation instrumentation for high-energy astrophysics.

Before joining UFG, I was a postdoctoral researcher at **Ruhr University Bochum** and **DESY** in Germany.
